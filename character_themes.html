"Your fury is supernatural. What drives your rage? What would calm it forever?"
Building Status (Intrinsic) - Rage

Count tags used, add Rage at that tier. Getting hurt or insulted adds Rage-1. May resist as reaction.

Rage 1-3: Adds to attacks & intimidation. Hinders careful/gentle.

Rage-4: MUST attack biggest threat. Devastating but unsubtle.

Rage-5: You ARE the Rage. Cannot tell friend from foe.

"The sword sings. What does it want? Do you serve the blade, or does it serve you?"
Pet/Harmony - Dissonance

The sword loves courage, hates cowardice, has opinions about honor.

In Harmony: Acting brave/honorable = +1 Power on attacks & parries.

Against Nature: Retreating, deception, cruelty = +1 Dissonance.

Dissonance-4: Sword acts on its own. Sings at wrong times, refuses to cut, strikes wrong targets.

Clear: Give the sword glory - one brave charge, one honorable choice.

"You decide where the line is. What will you never let pass? What would make you abandon your post?"
Commitment

Your defense only works where you commit. Half-measures = nothing.

Declare: At scene start, say what you protect: person, doorway, area, object.

While Committed: Defense is extraordinary. Stop impossible attacks. Hold against any odds.

Break: Moving or protecting something else breaks it COMPLETELY. Must declare again.

You cannot protect two things at once. Enemies WILL make you choose.

"Too fast, too clever, too bound. What promise weighs heaviest? What vow would you break?"
Binding Words

Every boast, promise, or challenge binds you IMMEDIATELY.

Making: "I'll defeat you" = can't retreat. "I won't hurt you" = can't attack.

Keeping: While doing what you promised = +1 Power.

Bound-4: Promises conflict and tangle. ("I'll protect you" + "I'll defeat him" = trouble when he grabs you.)

End: Fulfill exactly, or they release you from it.
