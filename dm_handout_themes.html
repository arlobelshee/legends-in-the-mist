Theme Magic Quick Reference

Building Status (Intrinsic)

Count power tags used → add status at that tier on self. Threshold=4, Limit=5.

Ragebound Berserker

Rage — Also +1 from hurt/insulted
@4: MUST attack biggest threat, devastating but unsubtle
@5: Can't tell friend from foe
Clear: -1 when combat ends + catch breath, or trusted ally calms you

Name-Binder

Strain — +1 tier for bigger changes (person vs object)
@4: Others can rename YOU and it works
@5: You become whatever others name you
Clear: -1 when called true name with meaning, or do something unmistakably you

Beast-Hearted

Ferality — Also +1 from hunting/fighting
@4: Think like animal: chase runners, fight challengers, protect pack
@5: Full beast, human concerns gone
Clear: -1 when civilized (share food politely, calm conversation, wait patiently)

Doppelganger

Confusion
@4: Slip between faces unintentionally, wrong voice at wrong time
@5: Forget which face is real, might BE the person imitated
Clear: -1 when time as true self doing something only real you would do

Building Status (Extrinsic)

Count power tags → add status at that tier to environment (or contain on self). Threshold=4.

Elemental Savant

Saturation — Environment gains element tags
@4 Env: Element dominates, GM gets major tags (living stone, howling wind, etc.)
@4 Self: Partial transform, +2 Power but think like element
@5 Self: Full transformation, ARE the element
Clear: -1 when opposite element appears or scene without using element

Blood of Giants

Shockwaves — Ground trembles, hinders precision for all
@4: Unstable environment: cracking floor, falling debris. All have trouble standing except them
Clear: -1/round when not stomping. New area = reset to 0

Trick-Spark

Mischief — Pranks/coincidences as tags, hinders serious actions for all
@4: Magic pranks EVERYONE: nothing where it should be, gravity optional
Clear: -1 when let magic play harmlessly (sparkles, harmless pranks)

Opposing Status

Count power tags → add status at that tier. Opposite axis cancels (remove lesser from greater).

Chaos Channeler

Chaos ↔ Order
@4: Reality weird: gravity suggestions, doors elsewhere, time hiccups
Clear: -1/scene, or impose structure/routine. Order from deliberate predictable actions.

Cryomancer

Cold ↔ Heat — Cold helps control/barriers, hinders speed/flexibility
@4: Freeze: treacherous ice, bitter silence. Movement hard except for them
Clear: -1/scene, faster with warmth

Pyromancer

Heat ↔ Cold — Heat helps destruction, hinders precision
@4: Wildfire: everything burning, smoke, escape cut off. Fire is own challenge
Clear: -1 when fires put out or move to unburned area

Songweaver

Fast Tempo ↔ Slow Tempo
Fast helps quick/bold, hinders careful. Slow helps calm/defense, hinders aggressive.
@4: Fast=frenzy, no time to think. Slow=time crawls, movements drag
Clear: -1/round. Complete silence clears all.

Shadowbound

Fading ↔ Presence — On self, not environment
Fading helps stealth, hinders being noticed/affecting physical world
@4: Barely there, hard to hurt but aid can't reach, forgotten mid-conversation
@5: Become shadow, sunlight agony, may not return
Clear: -1 in bright light or when grabbed + name spoken with feeling. Presence from light/contact/name.

Pet/Harmony

With nature = +1 Power. Against nature = +1 Strain/Dissonance. @4 it acts on its own.

Bearer of the Singing Sword

Dissonance — Sword loves courage, hates cowardice, opinions about honor
@4: Sword acts alone: sings when silence needed, refuses cuts, strikes own targets
Clear: -1 when let sword do what it wants (brave charge, honorable choice, moment of glory)

Beast Controller

Strain — Beast wants: hunt, protect territory, flee danger, eat
@4: Beast follows instincts: may flee, attack wrong target, refuse to move
Clear: -1 when let beast do one thing it wants (chase, eat, scare, nap)

Immediate Price

Pay NOW. Small magic = small price. Big magic = big price. Never permanent.

Merchant of Impossible Goods

1 tag: Shoelaces untie, hair color changes, voice squeaks
2+ tags: Sword→flowers, map goes blank, shoes swap, gravity funny
Universe prefers irony. Prices fade end of scene. Embarrassment permanent.

Immediate Demands

Spirit helps but demands payment NOW. Refuse = Annoyed. @4 = SO distracting.

Spirit Speaker

Annoyed (on spirits)
1 tag asks: Tell secret, break small thing, say name 3x
2+ tag asks: Give valued thing, do embarrassing thing, lend voice
@4: Spirits make noise, hide stuff, give bad advice loudly
Clear: -2 when finally give a spirit what it wants

Binding Words

Promises bind IMMEDIATELY. +1 Power while keeping. Each promise = +1 Bound.

Fey-Touched Duelist

Bound
"I'll defeat you" = can't retreat. "I won't hurt you" = physically can't attack.
@4: Promises conflict and tangle (protect you + defeat him = trouble when shield)
End: When fulfilled exactly OR other person releases you

Fragmentation

Creates copies/parts. Each = story tag. @3+ they may act on own or get targeted.

Mirror Adept

Reflections — In mirrors/water/polished surfaces. Can see through, swap, simple actions.
Breaking surface destroys reflection. Enemies learn to break mirrors.
@3+: Accidental swaps, reflections act alone, forget which is real
Clear: Dismiss reflections to reduce count

Living Shadow

Drift — Shadow can scout/manipulate/attack from darkness while body acts elsewhere
+1 Drift each separation or far-apart action
@4: Shadow pursues own goals, hides info, refuses to rejoin
Clear: -2 reunite in darkness. -1 feed shadow (scare someone, learn secret, swallow light)

Commitment

Declare what you protect. While committed = extraordinary defense. Moving/changing = breaks completely.

Iron Defender

Declare: person, doorway, area, object
While committed: stop attacks that bypass normal defense, hold impossible odds
Cannot protect two things at once. Enemies WILL make you choose.
