Theme Magic Quick Reference

Beast Controller

Pet/Harmony
Strain — Beast wants: hunt, protect territory, flee danger, eat
+1 Power with instinct. +1 Strain against instinct.
@4: Beast follows instincts: may flee, attack wrong target, refuse to move
Clear: -1 when let beast do one thing it wants (chase, eat, scare, nap)

Beast-Hearted

Building (Intrinsic)
Ferality — +1 from hunting/fighting
@4: Think like animal: chase runners, fight challengers, protect pack
@5: Full beast, human concerns gone
Clear: -1 when civilized (share food politely, calm conversation, wait patiently)

Bearer of the Singing Sword

Pet/Harmony
Dissonance — Sword loves courage, hates cowardice, opinions on honor
+1 Power in harmony. +1 Dissonance against nature.
@4: Sword acts alone: sings when silence needed, refuses cuts, strikes own targets
Clear: -1 when let sword do what it wants (brave charge, honorable choice)

Blood of Giants

Building (Extrinsic)
Shockwaves — Ground trembles, hinders precision for all
@4: Cracking floor, falling debris. All have trouble standing except them
Clear: -1/round when not stomping. New area = reset to 0

Chaos Channeler

Opposing (Chaos ↔ Order)
Chaos — Helps randomness/improv, hinders precision/planning
@4: Reality weird: gravity suggestions, doors elsewhere, time hiccups
Clear: -1/scene, or impose structure. Order from deliberate predictable actions.

Cryomancer

Opposing (Cold ↔ Heat)
Cold — Helps control/barriers, hinders speed/flexibility
@4: Freeze: treacherous ice, bitter silence. Movement hard except for them
Clear: -1/scene, faster with warmth

Doppelganger

Building (Intrinsic)
Confusion
@4: Slip between faces unintentionally, wrong voice at wrong time
@5: Forget which face is real, might BE the person imitated
Clear: -1 as true self doing something only real you would do

Elemental Savant

Building (Extrinsic or Intrinsic)
Saturation — Can redirect to environment OR contain on self
@4 Env: Element dominates (living stone, howling wind, etc.)
@4 Self: Partial transform, +2 Power but think like element
@5 Self: Full transformation, ARE the element
Clear: -1 when opposite element or scene without using element

Fey-Touched Duelist

Binding Words
Bound — Promises bind IMMEDIATELY. +1 Power while keeping.
"I'll defeat you" = can't retreat. "I won't hurt you" = can't attack.
@4: Promises conflict and tangle
End: When fulfilled exactly OR other person releases you

Iron Defender

Commitment
Declare what you protect: person, doorway, area, object
While committed: extraordinary defense, hold impossible odds
Moving or changing target = breaks completely
Cannot protect two things. Enemies WILL make you choose.

Living Shadow

Fragmentation
Drift — Shadow scouts/attacks from darkness while body acts elsewhere
+1 Drift each separation or far-apart action
@4: Shadow pursues own goals, hides info, refuses to rejoin
Clear: -2 reunite in darkness. -1 feed shadow (scare, learn secret, swallow light)

Merchant of Impossible Goods

Immediate Price
1 tag: Shoelaces untie, hair changes, voice squeaks
2+ tags: Sword→flowers, map blank, shoes swap, gravity funny
Universe prefers irony. Prices fade end of scene.

Mirror Adept

Fragmentation
Reflections — In mirrors/water/polished surfaces. See through, swap, simple actions.
Breaking surface destroys reflection.
@3+: Accidental swaps, reflections act alone, forget which is real
Clear: Dismiss reflections to reduce count

Name-Binder

Building (Intrinsic)
Strain — +1 tier for bigger changes (person vs object)
@4: Others can rename YOU and it works
@5: You become whatever others name you
Clear: -1 when called true name with meaning, or do something unmistakably you

Pyromancer

Opposing (Heat ↔ Cold)
Heat — Helps destruction, hinders precision
@4: Wildfire: everything burning, smoke, escape cut off
Clear: -1 when fires put out or move to unburned area

Ragebound Berserker

Building (Intrinsic)
Rage — +1 from hurt/insulted
@4: MUST attack biggest threat, devastating but unsubtle
@5: Can't tell friend from foe
Clear: -1 when combat ends + catch breath, or trusted ally calms you

Shadowbound

Opposing (Fading ↔ Presence) — on self
Fading — Helps stealth, hinders being noticed/affecting physical
@4: Barely there, hard to hurt but aid can't reach, forgotten mid-conversation
@5: Become shadow, sunlight agony, may not return
Clear: -1 in bright light or grabbed + name spoken with feeling

Songweaver

Opposing (Fast ↔ Slow Tempo)
Tempo — Fast helps quick/bold, Slow helps calm/defense
@4 Fast: Frenzy, no time to think
@4 Slow: Time crawls, movements drag
Clear: -1/round. Complete silence clears all.

Spirit Speaker

Immediate Demands
Annoyed (on spirits) — Spirit helps but demands payment NOW
1 tag asks: Tell secret, break small thing, say name 3x
2+ tag asks: Give valued thing, embarrassing act, lend voice
@4: Spirits make noise, hide stuff, give bad advice loudly
Clear: -2 when finally give a spirit what it wants

Trick-Spark

Building (Extrinsic)
Mischief — Pranks as tags, hinders serious actions for all
@4: Magic pranks EVERYONE: nothing where it should be, gravity optional
Clear: -1 when let magic play harmlessly (sparkles, harmless pranks)

System Reference

Building (Intrinsic): Count tags → status at that tier on self. @4=Threshold, @5=Limit. Can resist as reaction.
Building (Extrinsic): Count tags → status at that tier on environment. @4=Threshold. Can resist as reaction.
Opposing: Count tags → add status. Opposite pole cancels (lesser from greater). Can resist as reaction.
Pet/Harmony: With nature = +1 Power. Against nature = +1 Strain/Dissonance. @4 it acts on its own.
Immediate Price: Pay NOW. Small magic = small price. Big = big. Never permanent.
Immediate Demands: Entity helps but demands payment NOW. Refuse = Annoyed.
Binding Words: Promises bind IMMEDIATELY. +1 Power while keeping. Each = +1 Bound. @4 = conflict.
Fragmentation: Creates copies as story tags. @3+ may act on own or get targeted.
Commitment: Declare protection. Extraordinary defense while committed. Moving/changing = breaks completely.
